Wyatt Davis is a multi-disciplinary artist based in Orange County, California.

He has drawn on explorations in theater, dance, visual art, and clowning to develop a syncretic philosophy of art and life distilled in his songwriting.

He aims toward an integrated art practice rooted in wellness and community, and is currently focusing on songwriting, composing for Saddleback Theatre Dept's "Macbeth", and leading the OC Arts Collective "Weird Renaissance".
Artist Bio:

Songwriting is the most distilled expression of my being—
like everything that was never said crystallized inside of me,
and shown through with the light of my heart,
is seen dancing its reflections on the wall.
