Wyatt Davis is a multi-disciplinary artist based in Orange County, California.

He draws on explorations in theater, dance, visual art, and clowning to develop a syncretic philosophy of art and life, distilled through songwriting.

His work moves toward an integrated art practice rooted in wellness and community.

He is currently composing for Saddleback Theatre’s Macbeth and leading the OC arts collective Weird Renaissance.

Artist Bio

Songwriting is the most distilled expression of my being—
